About IWB

Founded in 2024, IWB is Lesotho's premier computer parts recycling company. We specialize in the responsible recycling of RAM, Hard Drives, and Motherboard components.

Our mission is to reduce electronic waste while recovering valuable materials that can be reused in manufacturing new products.

In addition to our recycling services, we've developed innovative software tools for data recovery and secure data destruction to help our clients manage their digital assets.
