Our Services

At IWB, we specialize in recycling computer parts with a focus on RAM, Hard Drives, and Motherboard components. Our eco-friendly processes ensure proper disposal while recovering valuable materials.

RAM Recycling

We safely recycle all types of RAM modules, recovering precious metals and reusable components.

  • DDR, DDR2, DDR3, DDR4, and DDR5 memory modules
  • Server and workstation RAM
  • Recovery of gold, silver, and palladium
  • Environmentally responsible disposal

Hard Drive Recycling

Secure data destruction and environmentally responsible recycling of all hard drive types.

  • HDD, SSD, and hybrid drives
  • Certified data destruction process
  • Recovery of rare earth magnets and metals
  • Certificates of destruction provided

Motherboard Recycling

Complete recycling of motherboard components including capacitors, chips, and connectors.

  • All motherboard types and generations
  • PCB recycling and precious metal recovery
  • Safe disposal of hazardous components
  • Component harvesting for repairs when possible

E-Waste Collection

Free collection of computer parts from businesses and individuals across Lesotho.

  • Scheduled pickups for businesses
  • Community collection events
  • Drop-off locations in major cities
  • Bulk collection services

Our Recycling Process

  1. Collection

    We collect e-waste from businesses, institutions, and individuals throughout Lesotho.

  2. Sorting & Assessment

    Components are sorted and assessed for potential reuse, repair, or recycling.

  3. Data Destruction

    For storage devices, data is securely erased or physically destroyed.

  4. Disassembly

    Components are carefully disassembled to separate valuable materials.

  5. Material Recovery

    Precious metals and other valuable materials are recovered through specialized processes.

  6. Responsible Disposal

    Any remaining materials are disposed of according to environmental regulations.
